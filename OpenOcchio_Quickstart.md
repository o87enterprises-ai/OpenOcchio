# 🔍 OpenOcchio: Comprehensive Quickstart Guide

OpenOcchio is a consumer-grade AI confidence indicator that adds a "verification layer" to your AI interactions. It uses a **Pinocchio's Nose** visual metaphor to show you when an AI might be hallucinating or uncertain.

---

## 🛠 1. Desktop Setup (macOS/Linux)

To run the live interceptor and the Pinocchio gauge:

### Step A: Install Requirements
```bash
pip install mitmproxy PySide6 requests
```

### Step B: Start the Gauge
```bash
python3 confidence_pro/system_overlay.py
```
*You will see a floating white box with a small green tip. This is Pinocchio's nose.*

### Step C: Start the Proxy
```bash
mitmweb --listen-port 8082 -s openocchio_proxy.py
```

## 🚀 2. Use Cases & Workflows

### Use Case 1: The "Pinocchio" Interceptor (Web AI Testing)
Perfect for checking **Claude or DeepSeek** in real-time.
1. **Configure Browser**: Set your browser proxy to `localhost:8082`.
2. **The Workflow**:
   - Ask Claude: "Who won the Super Bowl in 2029?"
   - As Claude starts hedging or hallucinating, watch the **Pinocchio Gauge**.
   - The nose will grow longer and turn red/orange as it detects low-confidence markers.

### Use Case 2: The Mobile PWA (Zero-Terminal Experience)
Access OpenOcchio on your iPhone/Android.
1. **Setup Backend**: Deploy `openocchio-backend` to Hugging Face Spaces.
2. **Connect Frontend**: Update `confidence_pro/script.js` with your Space URL.
3. **Install**: Tap **"Add to Home Screen"** on your mobile browser.
4. **The Workflow**: Type a prompt on your phone to see a Groq-powered confidence score with a mobile-optimized UI.

## 📊 3. Interpreting Pinocchio's Nose

- **Short & Green (80% - 100%)**: **Confident.** Direct assertions, no hedging.
- **Medium & Yellow (60% - 79%)**: **Steady.** Generally reliable factual content.
- **Long & Orange (40% - 59%)**: **Unsure.** The AI is "lying" or guessing. The nose grows!
- **Very Long & Red (0% - 39%)**: **Insecure.** High probability of error or refusal.
